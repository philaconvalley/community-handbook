Values aren't just words on a page — they're how we act when no one is watching and how we hold each other when things get hard. These are the values that define PhilaCon Valley.

---

## Belonging
Everyone who walks through the door belongs here. We actively create space for people who have been pushed to the margins of tech — not as a token gesture, but as a foundational commitment. You don't have to earn your place.

## Curiosity
We ask questions without shame and explore ideas without needing all the answers first. Curiosity is how we learn, how we build, and how we stay humble. We honor the parts of ourselves that never got space to wonder.

## Craft
We care about the quality of what we make. That doesn't mean perfection — it means intention. Whether it's code, a presentation, an event, or a conversation, we bring care to our work.

## Generosity
We share what we know. We open doors. We make introductions. We show up when someone needs a pair of eyes on their code or encouragement before a big interview. Generosity compounds — what we give comes back to the community.

## Accountability
We own our mistakes. We give feedback with honesty and receive it with openness. We follow through on commitments. When something goes wrong — with an event, a decision, a conversation — we address it directly instead of letting it fester.

## Joy
Tech can be joyful. Building something that works, learning something new, watching someone have a breakthrough — these moments matter. We protect space for celebration, laughter, and the genuine pleasure of creating.

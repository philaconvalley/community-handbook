# Changelog

All notable changes to the PhilaCon Valley Community Handbook are documented here.

---

## [1.0.0] — 2026-03-12

The handbook's first complete release. All core documents are drafted, reviewed for voice and consistency, and ready for community input.

### Added
- `community-guidelines.md` — Full code of conduct covering events, online spaces, reporting, and enforcement
- Website and LinkedIn links to README Connect section

### Changed
- `governance.md` — Expanded from a brief overview to a comprehensive governance document with organizer roles and areas of focus, layered decision-making process, pathway to becoming an organizer, community evolution and succession planning, and an honest treatment of power dynamics
- `README.md` — Added Community Guidelines to the Core Documents listing
- Versioned to 1.0.0 to mark the handbook as a complete first release

### Foundation (unchanged from initial draft)
- `mission.md` — Community mission statement
- `principles.md` — Guiding principles
- `community-values.md` — Community values

### Changed (Philosophy)
- `ai-principles.md` — Expanded from core principles to include transparency norms for AI use, guidance for members sharing AI-assisted work, and how the community itself uses AI in operations

### Philosophy (unchanged from initial draft)
- `builder-philosophy.md` — Builder-first philosophy
- `event-philosophy.md` — Event philosophy and format guide

### Meta
- `CONTRIBUTING.md` — Guide for community contributions
- `LICENSE` — MIT License

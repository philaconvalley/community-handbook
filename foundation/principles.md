These are the principles that guide how PhilaCon Valley operates — how we show up, make decisions, and grow together.

---

**1. Curiosity over credentials.**
You don't need a degree, a job title, or years of experience to belong here. We center learning, exploration, and the questions you haven't been given space to ask before.

**2. Build real things.**
We learn by doing. Workshops, hackathons, pair programming, projects — hands-on experience is how we grow. We build with each other, not for clout.

**3. Community before competition.**
We share resources, open doors, and celebrate each other's wins. The success of one member strengthens the whole community.

**4. Bring your full self.**
No code-switching. No gatekeeping. BIPOC and LGBTQIA+ folks are not guests here — this space was built for us.

**5. Philly first.**
We are rooted in Philadelphia. Our city's stories, culture, and people are part of what we're building. Local matters.

**6. Good vibes, real talk.**
We create space for both celebration and honesty. Positive energy doesn't mean avoiding hard conversations — it means we have them with care.

**7. Keep it open.**
The handbook, the community, the process — all of it should be transparent and accessible. If something isn't working, we say so and we fix it together.
